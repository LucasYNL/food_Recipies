Lucas's Amazing Burger Recipe


Absolutely mouth watering burger that will grant any tongue the sense of euphoria upon one single taste.

This holy burger is made with the simplest of ingredients to bring out the natural flavours of each ingredient. The process will be very simple, as the only cooking that needs to be done will be the burger patty itself. Follow my recipe and you will have a show-stomper of a burger for any occasion.


Ingredients

Portion per serving

  • 300g Medium-Lean Ground Beef
  • 1 Sesame Bun
  • Mayonnaise
  • Pickes
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Lettuce

Cooking Instructons

  1. Pre-heat frying pan to medium-high.
  2. Wash and cut lettuce into desired size.
  3. Shape the beef into a chubby patty and place into heated pan. Season to taste.
  4. Squish the patty after 1 mins and flip after 2 minutes.
  5. Cook the patty for another 3 minutes and prepare the buns, pickles, and cheese in the main time.
  6. Lettuce on bottom bun, patty on-top lettuce, followed by cheese, pickles, mayonnaise, and top bun.
  7. Plate and enjoy.
