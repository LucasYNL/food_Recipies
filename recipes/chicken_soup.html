Lucas's Amazing Chicken Soup Recipe


Absolutely mouth watering chicken soup that will grant any tongue the sense of euphoria upon one single sip.

This holy liquid is made with the simplest of ingredients to bring out the natural flavours of each ingredient. The process will be very simple, as the only cooking that needs to be done will be boiling all the ingredients. Follow my recipe and you will have a show-stomper of a chicken soup for any occasion.


Ingredients

Portion per serving

  • 200g Chicken Bones
  • White Radish
  • Gouji
  • Ginger
  • Spring Onions

Cooking Instructons

  1. Heat a soup pot with 500ml of water to boiling. Wash and clean chicken bones.
  2. Add chicken bones into pot and boil for 5 minutes.
  3. Remove chicken bones, wash and clean. Drain all liquid and clean pot.
  4. Heat 1 liter of water to boiling. Add in chicken bones and simmer on medium heat for 3 hours. Add water if bones start poking out of water.
  5. Peel and chop white radish into desired size. Chop 2 small slices of giner.
  6. Add radish, ginger into soup pot. Boil for another hour.
  7. Add spring onions and gouji. Boil for 10 minutes. Season with salt to taste.
  8. Plate and enjoy.
