Lucas's Amazing Fried Rice Recipe


Absolutely mouth watering fried rice that will grant any tongue the sense of euphoria upon one single taste.

This holy fried rice is made with the simplest of ingredients to bring out the natural flavours of each ingredient. The process will be very simple, as the only cooking that needs to be done will be stir-frying all the ingredients. Follow my recipe and you will have a show-stomper of a fried rice for any occasion.


Ingredients

Portion per serving

  • 100g SiChuan Dried Sausage
  • 300g Cooked Left-Over Rice
  • Spring Onions
  • 1 Egg
  • Sesame Oil

Cooking Instructons

  1. Pre-heat frying pan (wok-like if present) to high.
  2. Wash and cut spring onions and sausage into desired size and amount
  3. Microwave left-over rice for 30 seconds.
  4. Add cooking oil and crack in 1 egg. Stir the egg with cooking chopsticks to beat.
  5. Add in rice and sausage, toss stir for 1 minute. Add soy sauce to taste and toss for 20 seconds.
  6. Turn off heat, add in spring onions. Toss for 15 seconds.
  7. Add small amount of sesame oil and toss for 10 seconds
  8. Plate and enjoy.
